title: Why I'm writing here
description: What this site is for, and why the writing lives on my own domain rather than someone else's.
pubDate: 2026-07-05
---

This site exists mostly as plumbing. I wanted one canonical page that says who I am, links to everything I've made, and belongs to me rather than to an employer or a platform. The blog is the part of that plumbing that grows.

I already write on the Photoroom blog, and I'll keep doing that — posts about our systems belong there. But anything an employer's domain hosts ultimately accrues to the employer's domain. Writing that's mine — opinions, notes from side projects, things that outlast any one job — should live somewhere that moves with me. That's here.

Expect posts about backend systems, running coding agents in production, and the occasional note from building side projects like Savin Hood. Short and concrete, published when there's something worth saying rather than on a schedule.

If something here is useful, or wrong, email me — the address is on the front page.
